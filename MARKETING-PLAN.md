# Creami Recipes — Year 1 Marketing Plan

**Goal:** 30,000-80,000 monthly visitors by month 12, organic + social only (no paid ads).

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

### Search Engine Setup
- [ ] Submit sitemap to Google Search Console (`creamirecipies.com/sitemap-index.xml`)
- [ ] Submit sitemap to Bing Webmaster Tools
- [ ] Verify site ownership on both platforms
- [ ] Submit IndexNow pings for top 50 recipes (already have `/api/indexnow` endpoint)
- [ ] Request indexing for homepage, /recipes, /blog, and top 20 recipes manually in GSC
- [ ] Set up Google Search Console alerts for crawl errors

### Pinterest (highest-ROI channel for recipes)
- [ ] Create Pinterest Business account (@creamirecipies)
- [ ] Claim website and enable Rich Pins (recipe markup auto-generates Rich Pins)
- [ ] Create 10 boards by category: "Chocolate Ice Cream Recipes", "Healthy Creami Recipes", "Vegan Frozen Treats", "Sorbet Recipes", "Protein Ice Cream", "Creami Tips & Tricks", "Gelato Recipes", "Frozen Yogurt", "Creami for Beginners", "Seasonal Flavors"
- [ ] Design pin template in Canva (vertical 1000x1500, brand colors, recipe title + hero image)
- [ ] Pin 5-10 recipes/day for the first month (use a scheduler like Tailwind or Later)
- [ ] Every recipe pin links back to the recipe page
- [ ] Pin blog posts too — "10 Ingredients That Transform Your Creami" is perfect pin content

### Social Accounts
- [ ] Instagram (@creamirecipies) — visual platform, great for food
- [ ] TikTok (@creamirecipies) — Creami content does well on TikTok
- [ ] Facebook Page (Creami Recipes) — for sharing + community
- [ ] YouTube channel (optional, for later) — recipe video shorts

### Reddit Presence
- [ ] Join r/ninjacreami (45K+ members)
- [ ] Participate genuinely — answer questions, share tips
- [ ] When relevant, share a recipe link (community rules allow it if you're not spamming)
- [ ] Do NOT just drop links — build reputation first for 2-3 weeks
- [ ] r/icecreamery is another relevant sub

## Phase 2: Content Rhythm (Weeks 2-12)

### Pinterest (Primary Driver)
- Pin 3-5 recipes/day consistently (mix of new + catalog)
- Create "idea pins" (multi-image story pins) for step-by-step recipes
- Repin popular Creami content from others to build board authority
- Track which recipes get the most saves — double down on those categories
- **Target:** 100+ pins in first month, 500+ by month 3

### Instagram Strategy
- Post 3-4x/week
- Content mix:
  - Recipe cards (image + title overlay) → link in bio to recipe
  - Reels: 15-30s "how to make [recipe]" clips (even with just text + stock footage initially)
  - Stories: polls ("chocolate or vanilla?"), tips, behind-the-scenes
  - Carousel posts: "5 beginner mistakes" from blog content
- Use hashtags: #ninjacreami #creamirecipes #homemadeicecream #frozentreats #icecreammaker
- Engage with other Creami accounts — comment, share, collaborate

### TikTok Strategy
- Post 2-3x/week
- Content ideas:
  - "I tried making [flavor] in my Ninja Creami" format
  - Before/after: frozen puck → creamy ice cream
  - "3 ingredients, 5 minutes, better than store-bought"
  - Tips: "Stop making this mistake with your Creami"
  - Duet/stitch popular Creami videos
- Keep videos 15-60 seconds, hook in first 2 seconds
- Link to website in bio

### Blog Content (already scheduled)
- 1 post/week auto-publishes (104 posts scheduled)
- Share each post across all social channels on publish day
- Repurpose blog posts into social content:
  - Blog: "10 Ingredients That Transform Your Creami" → 10 individual Instagram posts
  - Blog: "5 Common Mistakes" → TikTok video series

### Email List (Start Collecting)
- [ ] Add email signup to site (simple "Get new recipes weekly" with Buttondown or ConvertKit free tier)
- [ ] Weekly digest email: 5 new recipes + 1 blog post
- [ ] This becomes your most valuable channel over time

## Phase 3: Growth & SEO Compounding (Months 3-6)

### SEO Optimization
- Monitor Google Search Console weekly:
  - Which queries are you ranking for?
  - Which pages have high impressions but low clicks? → Improve titles/descriptions
  - Which pages have crawl errors? → Fix immediately
- Target featured snippets:
  - Recipe FAQs are already structured for this
  - Blog posts with clear H2 questions + concise answers
- Internal linking:
  - "You May Also Like" section (already built) helps
  - Blog posts should link to specific recipes
  - Recipe descriptions can reference related recipes

### Pinterest Scaling
- By month 3 you should see which pins perform — create more of those
- Start group boards or Tailwind Communities for Creami/ice cream
- Seasonal content: ramp up summer recipes in March-April for May-September traffic

### Collaboration & Outreach
- Reach out to Creami-focused Instagram/TikTok creators for recipe swaps
- Offer to feature their recipes on your site (with credit + backlink exchange)
- Guest post on food blogs about Creami tips (backlinks help SEO enormously)
- Connect with Ninja/SharkNinja social — they often repost community content

### Facebook Groups
- Join: "Ninja Creami Recipes & Tips", "Ninja Creami Addicts", similar groups
- Share recipes when appropriate (most groups have share days)
- Answer questions with helpful tips + occasional link

## Phase 4: Authority & Monetization Prep (Months 6-12)

### SEO Authority Building
- Seek backlinks from:
  - Food bloggers who mention Creami
  - "Best Ninja Creami recipes" roundup posts — ask to be included
  - HARO (Help A Reporter Out) — respond to food/kitchen queries
- Create linkable assets:
  - "Ultimate Ninja Creami Guide" comprehensive page
  - "Ninja Creami Model Comparison" (people link to comparison content)
  - Infographics (shareable on Pinterest too)

### Content Expansion
- Add recipe video content (even simple overhead shots)
- User-generated content: repost when people make your recipes
- Seasonal recipe collections: "Summer Sorbets", "Holiday Ice Cream", "Back to School"

### Monetization Readiness
- By month 6-9 with 15K-40K monthly visitors:
  - [ ] Apply for Amazon Associates (need 3 sales in 180 days)
  - [ ] Apply for Mediavine (requires 50K sessions) or Raptive
  - [ ] If under 50K, start with Google AdSense or Ezoic (no minimum)
  - [ ] Activate ad zones already built into the site
- Affiliate content:
  - Fill in Amazon ASINs in blog posts
  - "Best [Product] for Ninja Creami" posts are high-intent, high-conversion
  - Review posts for Creami accessories, containers, ingredients
